Datapot Behavior Analysis System

People are the core of data leakage. According to the survey, 70% of enterprises worldwide have the risk of information leakage, and 55% of the data is leaked by internal employees. In recent years, information leakage incidents happen all the time.

Based on a lot of research on information leakage, Datapot launched a behavior analysis system. It uses machine learning and correlation analysis, through timeline comparison, statistical comparison of behavior, association of abnormal events, comparative indicators of abnormality, and the relationship between abnormal events and attack phases to automatically detect violations in real time. It can greatly reduce the probability of information leakage incidents.
