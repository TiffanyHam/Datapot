About Our Company

DataPot

Datapot INC. is a professional big data analytics service company. We provide the customers a user-behavior analysis platform, as well as professional consulting services and industry solutions services, dedicated to helping customers achieve data-driven solutions. Based on the frontiers of technology and rich experience in big data and user behavior analysis, our business now covers more than 10 major industries represented by the internet, finance, retails, high-tech, manufacturing, etc., and provides the cross-departmental support. We have a professional and highly intelligent service team to provide one-to-one customer service. Our key technologies in big data field include the massive data collection, storage, cleaning, analysis and mining, visualization, security and privacy protection.
Customers who have served include the world's top 500 Wal-Mart, China Mobile, Huawei, IBM and so on.
