  • Data center traffic is primarily in East-West traffic. In the meanwhile, the requirements of the rapid application deployment and mobility have been the challenges for the data center operations. The new Datapot Real-Time Big Data Network Security Analytics Visualization Platform uses machine learning, behavioral analysis and algorithms to provide comprehensive visibility and accurate identification of applications running in the data center and their dependencies:

    01

    Migrate applications from an existing legacy infrastructure to a programmable infrastructure;

    02

    Keep your infrastructure up-to-date to ensure disaster recovery;

    03

    Establish and deploy a consistent, accurate, and secure whitelisting strategy model;

    04

    Monitor the application's network policy compliance and identify any violations.

  • The Datapot platform is different from any other platform in the industry. It is a ready-to-use platform with advanced management capabilities that requires minimal configuration and is quick to deploy. Its machine learning capabilities significantly reduce the manual effort required to get the related information. In addition, its self-monitoring and self-diagnostics capabilities eliminate the big data expertise required to operate a cluster.

    Application Insights:Machine learning can be used to discover different applications and their communications in the data center. It automatically identifies and groups the different components, and generates the accurate dependency graphs.

    Policy recommendations:Policies can be generated for application layers and exported to other applications.

    Strategy Analysis:Supports the “try before use” mode, which performs simulation and impact analysis before applying the whitelisting strategy to the production environment.

    Compliance Audit:Monitors network policy compliance for application components. Any violation of the policy can be detected within a few minutes and a violation notification can be triggered.

    Visibility, troubleshooting, and investigation:Comprehensive traffic data can be collected and stored. Through the query, the visibility of the entire data center can be obtained. And the analysis and troubleshooting of the entire data center are also possible.
